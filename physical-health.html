Physical Health for Students

Tips para sa healthy na galaw, kain, at pahinga kahit toxic sa acads. Alagaan mo sarili mo, hindi lang puso mo ang deserve ng care.


  • 30 mins na activity? G na 'yan!
  • Walk or bike to class, sabay tayo?
  • Campus gym? Libre baka makita mo pa ako.
  • Study break? Stretching muna, usap after.
  • Workout, mental clarity, hindi excuse.
  • Join ka sa sports or fitness class, baka doon mo pa ako ma-meet.

Campus & Online Resources

Campus Recreation Centers

Karamihan sa universities may free or discounted access sa gym, fitness classes, at intramural sports para sa students.

Student Health Services

Pumunta sa health center ng campus para sa check-ups, consultations, at mga health education materials.

Fitness Apps

Maraming apps ang nag-aalok ng student discounts sa guided workouts, meditation, at sleep tracking tools.

Campus Nutritionist

Maraming universities ang nag aalok ng nutrition consultations sa pamamagitan ng student health services.
