About Our Mission

Our Story

Student Self-Care was created by a group of university students who recognized the critical need for accessible, practical self-care resources specifically designed for the unique challenges students face.

After witnessing firsthand how academic pressure, financial stress, and social adjustments were impacting their peers' wellbeing, our founding team decided to compile evidence-based strategies and practical advice into a comprehensive resource.

What started as a small campus initiative has grown into a full platform dedicated to supporting students through every aspect of their educational journey.
