Student Stress Management

Understanding Student Stress

    Student Stress means knowing what causes your stress as a student like exams, deadlines, pressure, or even group projects na ikaw lang ang gumagawa as usual . By recognizing these stressors, mas madali mo silang ma-handle. Di mo kailangan maging perfect, just aware and ready kasi mas nakakaganda 'pag calm and in control ka.

Recognizing Stress Signals

Awareness is the first step in managing stress. Learn to recognize how stress manifests for you:

Physical Signs

  • Headaches or muscle tension
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Fatigue or low energy
  • Digestive problems
  • Frequent colds or infections
  • Changes in appetite

Emotional Signs

  • Feeling overwhelmed or anxious
  • Irritability or short temper
  • Feeling down or hopeless
  • Lack of motivation
  • Mood swings
  • Difficulty relaxing

Cognitive Signs

  • Trouble concentrating
  • Racing thoughts
  • Forgetfulness
  • Negative thinking patterns
  • Poor judgment
  • Constant worrying

Behavioral Signs

  • Procrastination
  • Withdrawal from friends/activities
  • Increased use of caffeine, alcohol, or other substances
  • Nervous habits (nail biting, pacing)
  • Neglecting responsibilities
  • Change in normal routines
