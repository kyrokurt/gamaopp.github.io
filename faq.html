Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about student self-care, wellness, and our resources

What is student self-care and why is it important?

Student self-care refers to the intentional actions and practices students adopt to maintain their physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing while managing the demands of academic life.

How do I find time for self-care with a busy class schedule?

Finding time for self-care with a busy schedule requires intentional planning:


  • Schedule self-care activities in your calendar just like classes and assignments

  • Incorporate small self-care practices into existing routines.

  • Use time management techniques from our Time Management page
What are some quick stress management techniques for exam periods?
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts. Repeat 5 times.
  • 5-5-5 Grounding: Name 5 things you can see, 5 things you can hear, and 5 sensations you can feel.
  • 2-Minute Stretch Break: Stand up and stretch your neck, shoulders, and back to release tension.
  • Mindful Minute: Take 60 seconds to focus solely on your breathing.
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and then release each muscle group in your body.
  • Visualization: Briefly imagine a calm, peaceful place.

For more comprehensive stress management strategies, visit our Stress Management page.
